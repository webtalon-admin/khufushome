#### Key Investment Philosophy
- **Diversified, high-conviction strategy** with a flexible mandate across listed and unlisted markets
- Maintains ~25–30% in **unlisted assets** (infrastructure, property, private equity, private credit) — a major differentiator
- Employs a mix of **passive and active managers** alongside internal systematic teams
- "Whole-of-Fund" portfolio model with a standalone private markets division managing ~$47 billion
- Strong **responsible investment** integration; signatory to PRI and other frameworks
- London office opened Oct 2023 for expanded UK/European investment exposure
- Profit-to-member structure; no shareholders to pay

**Sources:** awareinvestments.aware.com.au; Investor Daily; Super Review; Markets Group

## DATA GAPS & NOTES

1. **Aware Super year-by-year returns (pre-FY2023):** Not publicly available in searchable format. The fund was formed in Jul 2020 from First State Super/VicSuper merger, and individual FY returns are not published on their website's performance page (which shows only annualised multi-year figures). Annual reports or member portal access needed.

2. **HESTA High Growth year-by-year returns (pre-FY2024):** The HESTA historical performance PDF contains this data but individual years are not easily extractable from web searches. The Balanced Growth year-by-year data was available via the MySuper Dashboard (a regulatory requirement for default options).

3. **HESTA Balanced Growth MySuper Dashboard returns vs headline returns:** The Dashboard returns (e.g., 9.88% for FY2025) are net of ALL fees including admin fees for a $50k balance. Headline returns (10.18%) are net of investment fees and tax only. This ~0.3% difference reflects admin fee deductions.

4. **Aware Super 3-year and 5-year returns to 30 Jun 2025:** Not separately disclosed for this exact date. The 5-year figure of 8.88% is to 31 Dec 2025; the 10-year of 9.58% is to 30 Sep 2025.

5. **Top holdings for HESTA:** Published as raw CSV files rather than curated top-10 lists. Requires download and analysis from hesta.com.au/our-portfolio-holdings.
