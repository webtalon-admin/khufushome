### 4. Fund Details

| Detail | Information |

| **New members FY25** | 415,000 (incl. 25,600 pension members) |
| **Fund inception** | August 1985 |
| **Morningstar sector** | Multisector Growth |
| **Benchmark** | SR50 Balanced (60–70) Index (median of top 50 super funds) |

#### Investment Philosophy (Four Pillars)

1. **Long-term investing** — Time horizons measured in years and decades to support wealth creation
2. **Active management** — Identifying assets, sectors, and markets believed to outperform; integrated ESG considerations
3. **Global approach** — Investing in best opportunities and talent worldwide
4. **One Fund strategy** — Leveraging collective scale and skills across the organisation; profit-for-member structure means all returns flow back to members

#### Investment Philosophy

- **Growth-biased diversification** — Portfolio intentionally tilted toward growth assets (75/25 split)
- **Significant alternatives exposure** — One of the highest allocations to private equity, venture capital, and alternatives in the Australian super industry (~32%)
- **Long-term horizon** — 20-year rolling return target reflects genuinely long-term thinking
- **Multi-manager approach** — Engages diverse specialist managers across asset classes; uses JANA as external asset consultant
- **Profit-for-member** — All investment returns flow back to members
