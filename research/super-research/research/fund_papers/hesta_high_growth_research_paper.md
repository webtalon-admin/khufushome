## 2. Investment Philosophy & Strategy

HESTA's investment approach is built around **"Super with Impact"**—a philosophy that integrates responsible investment with long-term wealth creation. The fund operates under five core beliefs: that responsible investment enhances risk-adjusted returns; that active ownership through engagement and proxy voting drives better outcomes; that climate and sustainability factors are material to performance; that diversity and inclusion strengthen decision-making; and that transparency builds member trust. HESTA is a signatory to the Principles for Responsible Investment (PRI) and systematically integrates ESG considerations across its portfolio.

The fund's sector focus—health and community services—aligns with its membership base and informs its engagement priorities. HESTA uses active ownership to influence companies on climate transition, labour practices, and governance, rather than relying solely on screening. This approach allows the fund to maintain a broad investable universe while still advancing sustainability goals. The High Growth option's 82/18 growth/defensive split reflects a higher risk tolerance suited to members with longer investment horizons who can withstand short-term volatility.

HESTA's scale—over $100 billion in assets and more than one million members—enables access to institutional-quality investments, competitive fee negotiation, and in-house expertise. As a profit-for-member industry fund, all returns flow back to members. The fund has received the SuperRatings Net Benefit Award 2026 and ranks in the Top 10 across multiple options over 10 years, reflecting strong long-term outcomes for members.

**Growth / Defensive split:** 82% growth / 18% defensive

### Commentary

- **Strong equity tilt:** 63.5% in Australian and international shares provides significant growth exposure; the 82/18 split is among the most growth-oriented options in the industry.
- **Meaningful unlisted exposure:** 17% combined in infrastructure (10%) and property (7%) offers diversification and potential illiquidity premia; 8% private equity adds further alternatives exposure.
- **Modest defensive allocation:** 4.5% global debt and 5% cash provide limited downside cushion; suitable for members comfortable with volatility.
- **Global diversification:** International shares (31.5%) nearly match Australian allocation (32%), reducing home bias.
