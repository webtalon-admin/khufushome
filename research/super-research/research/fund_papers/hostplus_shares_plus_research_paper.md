## 2. Investment Philosophy & Strategy

Hostplus Shares Plus is a **near-100% growth** option designed for members seeking maximum long-term returns with minimal defensive allocation. The 96/4 growth/defensive split means zero fixed interest and zero cash—a stark contrast to the Balanced option's ~6% defensive allocation. This structure maximises exposure to equity and alternative growth assets.

The option shares Hostplus's multi-manager approach and alternatives exposure (~24% in alternatives), but tilts heavily toward listed equities: ~32% Australian and ~40% international shares. Combined with property/infrastructure and alternatives, the portfolio is almost entirely growth-oriented. The philosophy aligns with members who have long investment horizons and can tolerate significant volatility—including negative years—in pursuit of higher returns.

Shares Plus has delivered the strongest long-term returns among Hostplus options: 10.26% p.a. over 10 years vs 9.00% for Balanced. However, this comes with higher volatility. The option is suitable for members with 15+ year horizons who understand that downside risk is amplified when defensive assets are absent.

## 7. Key Risks & Considerations

| Risk | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Maximum volatility** | Near-100% growth assets; expect larger drawdowns than Balanced or AustralianSuper in downturns. |
| **Zero defensive cushion** | No fixed interest or cash; no buffer in equity sell-offs. |
| **FY2022-type events** | In severe market downturns, Shares Plus would likely fall more than Balanced (-1.45%) or AustralianSuper (-2.97%). |
| **Concentration** | Heavy equity exposure; sector concentration (e.g., tech) can amplify losses. |
| **Horizon** | Suitable only for 15+ year horizons; short-term investors risk crystallising losses. |
| **Smaller option** | $7B AUM vs $90B+ for Balanced; less scale but still substantial. |
